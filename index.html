Semantic Elements Paragraph
"Semantics is the study of the meaning of words and phrases in a language."

Therefore, within HTML, semantic elements are elements people use to convey
meaning to the browser and developer. There is a multitude of elements that
can be used within HTML. Only elements that clearly conveys meaning effectively
to the browser as well as the developer are considered semantic elements.
